# **App Name**: CinnamonLink Pro

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Authorization: Secure login functionality for Admin and Staff users, implementing role-based access control across the application.
- Centralized Business Dashboard: An interactive dashboard displaying key business metrics such as total customers, total sales, total revenue, recent invoices, and low stock alerts for quick oversight.
- Customer Relationship Management (CRM): Comprehensive customer management with functionalities to add, edit, and delete customer profiles, track contact details, purchase history, and manage customer notes.
- Product & Inventory Control: Management system for products including adding, editing, and deleting items with SKU, price (USD/LKR), category, images, and real-time stock quantity tracking with low stock warnings.
- Invoice Generation & Sales Tracking: Feature to create, view, and manage invoices with automatic numbering, product selection, quantity/total calculation, tax, shipping, and the ability to track sales history and status.
- Actionable Customer Insights: An AI tool to analyze customer notes and purchase history to provide data-driven suggestions for customer classification (e.g., VIP/Regular) and proactive engagement strategies.
- Essential Business Reporting & Export: Generate fundamental business reports (e.g., monthly/yearly sales, top products, top customers) with filtering options and the ability to export data to CSV format.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A warm, earthy sienna (#B4673D) evoking the essence of cinnamon and professionalism, chosen to stand out subtly on a light background.
- Background color: A very light, inviting cream (#FAF7F5) with a hint of warmth, ensuring readability and a clean, expansive feel for data presentation.
- Accent color: A vibrant rose (#E6687C) to highlight important calls to action or notifications, offering a dynamic contrast to the primary and background tones.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter', a modern sans-serif, provides excellent readability and a clean, objective feel suitable for a business management application.
- Utilize a consistent set of clean, line-based vector icons with minimal fills. Icons should intuitively represent business concepts (e.g., charts for dashboard, user profiles for CRM) for clear navigation.
- A classic responsive layout featuring a fixed-position left sidebar for main navigation and a clean top header for global actions and branding. The main content area will be adaptable to various screen sizes, employing a modular grid for dashboard elements and tabular data.
- Incorporate subtle and swift UI transitions for navigation, form submissions, and data loading states. Implement micro-interactions such as gentle hovers on actionable elements and smooth table row selections to enhance user experience without distraction.
